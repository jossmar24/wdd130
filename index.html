Quito-Ecuador

Ecuador is a South American country of geographical and cultural contrasts, located on the equator. With an area of ​​283,561 km², it covers four distinctive regions: the Pacific coast, the Andes mountain range, the Amazon and the Galapagos Islands. Its population of approximately 17 million is a diverse mix of ethnic groups, including mestizos, indigenous people and Afro-Ecuadorians. Spanish is the official language, but Kichwa and Shuar are also recognized. (LDS Church)
